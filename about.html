How it started?

It all started in Bhopal with a single vision: to bridge the gap between cinematic storytelling and digital performance. We began as a small team of techies and creatives who saw that brands weren't just looking for videos—they were looking for identities.

Today, BerryMint Co. stands as a hybrid powerhouse, blending the high-end aesthetics of a production house with the data-driven precision of a marketing agency.
